Portfolio

Data Science

Maternal and Neonatal Health & Malaria PowerBI Dashboards

I applied R programming tools to analyze and visualize complex health data, providing actionable insights for maternal, neonatal, and malaria-related initiatives. The Maternal and Neonatal Health dashboard, in particular, was created for the $30 million Beginnings Fund investment in Zimbabwe and utilized Bayesian statistics for more robust analysis. The malaria dashboard was created using simulation data.
